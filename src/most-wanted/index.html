Most Wanted Records

Everyone who collects vinyl has a list. This list may be written down, catalogued in an app, or just a nebulous idea floating about in their head. I am, of course, referring to the namesake of this experiment: the "most wanted" list.

While some factors besides enjoyment or replayability of a record play into desire for physical media (rarity probably being the most prominent), I wanted to see if I could quantify exactly which records should be on my list based on those factors. To do so, I first grabbed a list of my records from Discogs and normalized the response. Then, I requested my most played albums from Last.fm, and displayed the first 25 I didn't already own on physical media on this very page.
